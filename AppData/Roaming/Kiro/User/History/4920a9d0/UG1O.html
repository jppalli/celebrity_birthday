How to Play

Objective

Unscramble the letters to fill in the missing words and reveal the author to complete the inspirational quote.

How to Play

  1. Look at each scrambled word (like "TAERG")
  2. Unscramble the letters to form a real word
  3. Type your answer in the input field
  4. The last scramble is always the author's name
  5. Click "Check Solution" when you're ready

Tips & Strategy

  • Use the "Get a Hint" button if you're stuck
  • Think about common words that fit the quote context
  • The author is usually a famous person
  • All letters in the scramble must be used exactly once

Scoring

Complete the puzzle to maintain your daily streak. Your stats track puzzles solved, current streak, and average completion time.
