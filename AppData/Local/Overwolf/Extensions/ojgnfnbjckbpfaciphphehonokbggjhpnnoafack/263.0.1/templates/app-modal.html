Home of your highlights and in-game analytics

The wait is over! Apex Legends is here. Get Game Summary - your one stop shop for game highlights and post-game analytics!

The app tracks your games, and presents you with automatic and manual captured highlights ready to be shared. The app will even tell you about awesome events and competitions you can join.

Beyond highlights, Game Summary offers you a slew of useful analytics and in-game stats to research and explore - from actions per minute (APM), through Kill/Death ratio, to relative performance, you can learn how to improve your game easily and quickly using the Game Summary app.

This is just the beginning, and Game Summary is being upgraded with new features and more advanced analytics constantly - there is plenty to look forward to!


The Overwolf Game Summary supports the following games:

  • League of Legends
  • CS:GO
  • Apex Legends
  • Hearthstone
  • Fortnite
  • PUBG
  • Dota 2
  • Rainbow 6 Siege
  • World of Tanks
  • World of Warships
  • Splitgate
  • Rocket League
