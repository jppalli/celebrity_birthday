IMPORTANT REMINDERS

Thank you for choosing Nature Air.

AIRPORT CHECK-IN

  • Nature Air is located at Juan Santamaria Airport (SJO), 20 km west of downtown San Jose.
  • Check-in is 1 hour prior to scheduled departure for flights departing from San Jose airport. Flights are closed 45 minutes before departure.
  • Check-in is 2 hour prior to scheduled departure for flights to/from Bocas del Toro, Panama and Managua, Nicaragua. And close 1 hour before departure.
  • Check-in from local destinations is 45 minutes prior to scheduled departure. Flights are closed 30 minutes before departure.


    • To all passengers not timely checked in, will not be boarded on the aircraft.

  • Valid Passport for foreigners must be presented at all airports to guarantee boarding, and valid Government ID for Costa Ricans and residents must be presented as well.
  • All passengers with an international flight to the Juan Santamaria Airport in San Jose must carry printed proof of onward travel from Costa Rica (generally a flight or bus reservation departing within 90 days). The Costa Rican Department of Immigration requires passengers to present this proof along with their travel documents in the airport in Managua/Bocas del Toro to guarantee boarding. The same applies for flights to Managua/Bocas del Toro (passport and proof of onward travel must be presented in Costa Rica before boarding your international flight).
  • Nature Air will do not accept cash payments in any or our Airport locations. Payment for additional seats, overweight baggage, etc. must be made using a Debit or Credit Card. The only exceptions would be in San Jose, SJO Airport where cash can be deposited at an onsite bank.
  • Any passenger traveling on NATURE AIR with a body weight of more than 250 pounds must purchase an extra seat. This same person will have the right to bring the amount of luggage allotted for both seats purchased, plus an additional 10 pounds as carry-on.

BAGGAGE POLICY

Checked Baggage:

  • All passengers have the right to check one bag with a maximum size not exceeding 50 linear inches / 127 cm. (length + width + height). Any luggage exceeding weight allowance (varies by fare type, see below) and/or size will be subject to space availability and charged according to the excess luggage fees below. Over-sized luggage or bags exceeding 60 pounds will be charged as cargo and subject to space availability. Baggage allowances apply depending on the fare type selected:
    • FlexFare: 40 pound allowance for checked baggage
    • PromoFare: 27 pound allowance for checked baggage
    • LocosFare: 15 pound allowance for checked baggage
  • Excess luggage must be paid for at the airport prior to travel. The following fees apply:
    • 1 to 5 pounds over limit = $7 charge.
    • 6 to 15 pounds over limit = $20 charge.
    • 16 to 30 pounds over limit = $40 charge.
    • 31 to 60 pounds over limit = $60 charge.
  • Surfboards, golf clubs, fishing equipment, baby car seats, bicycles, musical instruments and other items are subject to space availability at the time of check in. Charges for these articles is US$30 per flight.
  • Payment of excess baggage fees does not guarantee baggage will be transported on the same flight as the passenger, and may be put on next available flight. Collection of late arriving baggage is the responsibility of the passenger. All baggage is subject to space availability.
  • Nature Air do not transport hazard materials (for more information please check terms and conditions on our website www.natureair.com)

Carry-On Baggage:
  • Allpassengers may bring one piece of carry-on luggage as long as it does not exceed the 10 lbs. weight limit and a maximum size not exceeding 33 linear inches / 84 cm. (length + height + width). Any carry-on luggage exceeding the maximum weight and/or size will be considered and charged as excess luggage.
  • Nature Air is not responsible for personal property left inside the aircraft cabin.
  • Nature Air does not transfer baggage between flights operated by any other airline, transportation company or international flights.
  • All baggage is subject to space availability.

NON REFUNDABLE POLICY

  • All reservations are non-refundable. No exceptions will be made to this policy.
  • No Show passengers lose 100% of the ticket purchased.
  • Changes may be made to reservations commensurate with the policies of the fare purchased:
    • FlexFares: Most flexibility. Itinerary, date of departure and time of flight can be changed up to 24 hours before departure for a fee of USD $15.00. If done within 24 hours, penalty is USD $25.00.
    • PromoFares: Some flexibility. Itinerary, date and time of flight can be changed up to 24 hours before departure when the new fare is of equal or lesser value for a US$40 fee. The difference in fares will be charged in addition to US$40 penalty if available ticket price is higher. No changes allowed within 24 hours.
    • LocosFares: Most restricted. Itinerary, date and time of flight cannot be changed at any time.
